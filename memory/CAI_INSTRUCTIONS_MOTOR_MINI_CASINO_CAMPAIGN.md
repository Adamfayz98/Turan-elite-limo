## 💰 BIDDING & BUDGET SCHEDULE

### Days 1–3 (Learning phase)
- **DO NOT** touch budget or bids
- **DO NOT** pause/enable/edit any keywords
- **DO NOT** turn on Target CPA
- Let Google's Smart Bidding gather baseline data

### Days 4–14
- Review the **Search Terms Report** every 2–3 days
- Add irrelevant search terms as **negative keywords** at the ad group level (NOT the campaign level unless it's clearly a bad term for all 3 ad groups)
- Common expected additions: `used bus`, `bus for sale`, `bus driver salary`, `bus dimensions`, `charter bus tickets`
- Ignore Quality Score for the first 14 days (needs time to stabilize)

### Days 15–30 (Optimization phase)
- If **total conversions across all 3 ad groups ≥ 30**, switch bidding strategy to **Target CPA**:
  - Motor Coach ad group: Target CPA = **$85**
  - Mini Coach ad group: Target CPA = **$60**
  - Casino Charter ad group: Target CPA = **$40**
- If total conversions < 30 after 14 days, keep Maximize Conversions and increase daily budget by 25% (to $75/day)
- Pause any keyword with **>50 impressions and 0 clicks after 7 days**
- Pause any keyword with **>10 clicks and 0 conversions after 14 days**

### Weekly review checklist (owner will run this every Monday)
1. Search Terms Report → add negatives
2. Top-of-page bid rate (target: 40%+)
3. Impression share lost due to budget (if >20%, increase budget on winning ad groups)
4. Impression share lost due to rank (if >30%, ad relevance is weak — check landing page match)

## 🆘 IF GOOGLE FLAGS AN AD

**Most likely rejection reasons:**
1. **Gambling promotion** (Casino Charter ad group) — appeal with: "Transportation service to a licensed venue. Not a gambling advertiser. No promotion of gambling. Certified TCP carrier under CA state law."
2. **Insurance/legal claim** ($5M Insurance mention) — provide the certificate of insurance if requested. It's on file.
3. **Trademark violation** (mentioning a casino by name in headlines) — remove specific casino names from headline; keep them only in descriptions where allowed.

If any ad is rejected and appeal is denied, DO NOT force it — remove the flagged keyword/headline and continue with the rest.
