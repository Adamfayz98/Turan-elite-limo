**Weekly review checklist:**
1. Search terms report → add irrelevant queries as negatives

3. Impression share lost due to budget → increase daily budget on winners

## What to Do NOW (in the Google Ads UI)

1. **Create the campaign** with the settings above
2. **Create Ad Group 1 (Motor Coach)** — paste keywords, ads, extensions
3. **Create Ad Group 2 (Mini Coach)** — same
4. **Create Ad Group 3 (Casino Charter)** — same
5. **Set daily budget** to $45–$75 total
6. **Add campaign-level negative keywords** (from Ad Group 1's list — they apply across all)
7. **Enable auto-tagging** in Google Ads → Settings → Account settings → so `gclid` is passed to your site
8. **Verify** by clicking your ad from the "Ad Preview & Diagnosis" tool → confirm you land on the right page with `gclid` in URL
9. **Wait 24 hours** — do NOT touch bids or budgets for the first 3 days (Google's learning phase)
10. **Day 4+**: review search terms daily, add negatives, refine keywords
