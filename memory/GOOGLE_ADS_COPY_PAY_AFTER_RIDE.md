**Date:** 2026-02
**Owner:** Marketing / CAI (Ad Manager)
**Objective:** Reduce Stripe checkout drop-off by leading every ad with the "no card charged today" promise, and highlighting Apple Pay / Google Pay availability.

This document contains ready-to-paste Responsive Search Ad (RSA) headlines, descriptions, callouts, and sitelinks. Copy them directly into Google Ads Editor — no rewording needed.

## 7) Promotion Extension (top-tier CPC lift)

- **Occasion:** *Leave blank* (evergreen)
- **Item:** "Book Now, Pay After Your Ride"
- **Percent off:** *Leave blank* — this is NOT a discount, it's a payment-timing promise
- **Details:** "No card charged today · Card securely verified & saved by Stripe · Charged only after your ride is completed. Applies to Executive Sedan, Luxury SUV, First Class."
- **Final URL:** `https://turanelitelimo.com/#booking`

⚠️ Because Google's Promotion Extension is designed for discount codes, we may need to instead surface this via the **Callouts** and **Sitelinks** rather than the Promotion Extension. Test both — some accounts allow non-discount promo copy through, others reject it.

## 9) Deployment Checklist

1. [ ] Copy Headlines + Descriptions above into a fresh **Responsive Search Ad** in the "Executive Sedan", "Airport", and "Corporate" ad groups.
2. [ ] Set **"Book Now, Pay After Your Ride"** as pinned Headline 1 (position 1) so it always shows first.
3. [ ] Add all Callouts at the account level so they inherit across every campaign.
4. [ ] Update all existing RSAs' asset text — Google will A/B test the new copy against your existing headlines and gradually favor the winners.
5. [ ] Do NOT delete existing high-performing ads immediately — pause them AFTER the new ones accumulate 1 week of data.
6. [ ] Monitor CTR week-over-week. Expected lift: +15-30% CTR based on the strength of the payment-timing promise.
7. [ ] Cross-check Conversion Actions in Google Ads to ensure `Booking_Completed` still fires from Stripe success URLs (`/thank-you?bid=...`) — new copy shouldn't affect this, but confirm.

## 10) Landing Page Alignment

The website and landing pages have been updated to match this ad copy — the "Book Now · Pay After Ride" pill now appears:
- Above the fold on the homepage hero
- On every eligible vehicle card in the fleet picker
- On the Wedding, Wine Tour, Airport, and Corporate landing pages
- On the booking form's payment step
- On the Stripe checkout page itself (via `custom_text[submit][message]`)

This ensures **ad copy → landing page → checkout** all reinforce the same promise, which is critical for Google's Ad Rank & Quality Score.
